Welcome to STCTM's Documentation!
=================================

STCTM: the STellar ConTamination Modeling framework

**stctm** performs spectral retrievals on **exoplanet transmission spectra** and (out of transit) **stellar spectra** assuming the stellar surface can be explained by a combination of spectral components.
